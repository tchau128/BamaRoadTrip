# Day 2 — Monday, July 20

⬅ [[Day 1 - Sunday July 19|Day 1]] | ➡ [[Day 3 - Tuesday July 21|Day 3]]

## Early morning — Side trip: Bamahenge, Elberta
Full-size fiberglass Stonehenge — about 1.5 hrs each way across the bay near Gulf Shores. Adds roughly 2.5–3 hrs round trip from Bayou La Batre. Easiest thing to cut if the day feels too packed.

## Midday — Bayou La Batre docks & shrimp boats
The real-life Bubba/Forrest Gump shrimping town — Shell Belt Road is the classic photo stretch. No formal tour desk here — call local eco-tour/charter operators ahead, or just walk the docks when boats come in (mid-morning).

## Afternoon — Bayou La Batre Museum
Small town history: shipbuilding, shrimping, Vietnamese refugee community.

## Late afternoon — Africatown, Mobile
Founded by the last Africans smuggled illegally on the Clotilda; Africatown Heritage House museum.
