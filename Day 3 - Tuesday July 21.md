## Storm Status — Tropical Storm Bertha
*(as of morning 7/21)*
- Center offshore & to the SE — I'm on the wet/gusty/building-surf side, not the core
- ~50 mph winds, peaking ~60 today; moving NW ~5 mph
- Tropical storm warning along the whole coastal route
- Surge up to 4 ft possible; 2–4 in rain, isolated 6 in
- Tracking toward Louisiana landfall late Wed/Thurs
- **Decision:** don't overnight on the coast — drive past Gulf Shores and pull inland ahead of the evening rain

### Safety notes (how it played out)
- Called ahead — Fort Morgan ferry was running
- Checked AL-193 bridge before crossing (ALGO Traffic)
- Skipped low/exposed West End Beach — surge-prone
